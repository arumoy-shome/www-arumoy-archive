This document tracks the goals I wish to accomplish. Goals and habits go hand in hand. I usually employ deliberate practise through sustainable habits to achieve my goals.

I categorise goals based on estimated duration of completion: 5 years, 1 year, 4 months and 1 month. 5 year goals are sparse and very broad. They usually consist of significant life accomplishments or career changes. 5 year goals are often made up of several smaller goals.

1 year goals are more typical and annually reviewed/refreshed. 4 months and 1 month goals are typically employed to field test the sustainability of a goal through habit formation.

Goals with a TODO status are the ones I am currently pursuing.
